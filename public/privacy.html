Privacy Policy for Seshio Bot

Last updated: Today

1. Data We Collect

Seshio Bot collects only the minimum amount of data required to function properly. This includes:

  • Guild (Server) IDs: To save server-specific settings.
  • Role IDs: To know which roles to ping during sessions.
  • User IDs: Temporarily stored during active voting to prevent double-voting.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is strictly used for the operational mechanics of the bot (e.g., starting sessions, managing votes). We do not use your data for analytics, tracking, or marketing.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. All data is securely stored in our database.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact the bot owner via Discord.
